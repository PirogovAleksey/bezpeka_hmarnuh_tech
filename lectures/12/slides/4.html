Тема 12.4: Vendor Selection та майбутнє

Vendor Evaluation

  • Functional: Coverage, detection, remediation
  • Non-Functional: Performance, scalability
  • Operational: Deployment, maintenance
  • Commercial: Pricing, TCO, viability
  • POC: 2-4 weeks з success criteria

Build vs Buy

  • Buy: Commodity, rapid need, limited team
  • Build: Unique requirements, competitive edge
  • Hybrid: Platform + custom automation
  • Open source + commercial support
  • Consider TCO, not just license cost
