Тема 12.4: Vendor Selection та майбутнє

Vendor Evaluation

  • Functional: Coverage, detection, remediation
  • Non-Functional: Performance, scalability
  • Operational: Deployment, maintenance
  • Commercial: Pricing, TCO, viability
  • POC: 2-4 weeks з success criteria

Build vs Buy

  • Buy: Commodity, rapid need, limited team
  • Build: Unique requirements, competitive edge
  • Hybrid: Platform + custom automation
  • Open source + commercial support
  • Consider TCO, not just license cost

Emerging Trends

  • AI-Native: LLMs для investigation, remediation
  • Platform Engineering: Security as self-service
  • Zero Trust Maturity: Identity-centric everywhere
  • Supply Chain: SBOM, Sigstore, SLSA
  • Quantum-Safe: Post-quantum cryptography

Career Development

  • Certs: CCSP, CCSK, AWS/Azure Security
  • Hands-on: Labs, CTF (flAWS, CloudGoat)
  • Community: CSA, OWASP, fwd:cloudsec
  • Follow thought leaders, blogs, podcasts
  • Contribute to open source

Підсумки курсу

  • Foundations: NIST, Shared Responsibility
  • Architecture: Zero Trust, microsegmentation
  • Workloads: VM, containers, serverless
  • Tools: CSPM, CNAPP, CWPP, SIEM
  • Operations: Detection, response, improvement

Final Thoughts

  • Defense in Depth: Багато шарів захисту
  • Least Privilege: Мінімальні права
  • Assume Breach: Готуйтесь до компрометації
  • Continuous: Безпека — процес, не продукт
  • Дякую за проходження курсу!
